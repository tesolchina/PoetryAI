# PoetryAI Pilot Testing - Reflection Form

## Introduction

Thank you for participating in the PoetryAI pilot testing! Your feedback is invaluable in helping us refine the system before the main study.

This reflection form is designed to gather your honest thoughts, observations, and experiences during the testing session. Please answer all questions as thoroughly and candidly as possible. There are no right or wrong answers – we want to understand your genuine experience with the PoetryAI platform.

The form is divided into 9 parts covering different aspects of your testing experience:
- **Parts 1-3:** Technical functionality (login, chatbot interaction, chat history features)
- **Parts 4-5:** User experience and overall impressions
- **Parts 6-7:** Technical issues and improvement suggestions
- **Parts 8-9:** Overall assessment and additional comments

**Estimated completion time:** 20-30 minutes

Please complete this form immediately after your testing session while your experience is fresh in your mind.
