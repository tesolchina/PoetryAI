## üéØ Screening Criteria

Use this checklist when reviewing responses to select participants:

### Required Criteria (Must Have):
- ‚úÖ Age 18+
- ‚úÖ English proficiency: Intermediate or higher
- ‚úÖ Available for both sessions
- ‚úÖ Has necessary device and internet
- ‚úÖ Preliminary consent given

### Ideal Participant Mix (aim for diversity):
- Mix of L1 and L2 English speakers (prefer L2 for study focus)
- Range of poetry experience (beginners to intermediate)
- Range of AI tool familiarity (not professional users)
- Balance across demographic categories

### Exclusion Criteria:
- ‚ùå Professional poets/writers
- ‚ùå Low English proficiency (if can't communicate comfortably)
- ‚ùå Extensive AI writing tool usage (daily professional use)
- ‚ùå Cannot access required technology

## üîê Data Privacy Notes

**Important reminders:**

1. **Store securely:** Keep the response spreadsheet password-protected
2. **Limit access:** Only research team members should access raw data
3. **Anonymize early:** Assign participant IDs (P1-P4) immediately upon selection
4. **Separate storage:** Keep contact info separate from research data
5. **Delete timeline:** Specify when raw identifying data will be deleted (e.g., 6 months post-study)
6. **Backup:** Maintain secure backup of responses
