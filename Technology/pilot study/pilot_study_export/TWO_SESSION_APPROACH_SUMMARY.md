# Two-Session Pilot Testing Approach
## Summary of Changes

---

## üîÑ What Changed?

The pilot testing has been restructured from **one 60-minute session** into **two separate shorter sessions**:

### Original Approach (Single Session)
- ‚ùå One 60-minute session per participant
- All activities in one sitting
- Risk of participant fatigue
- Technical issues could derail entire session

### NEW Approach (Two Sessions)
- ‚úÖ **Session 1**: 10 minutes - Login & Setup
- ‚úÖ **Session 2**: 25 minutes - Poetry Writing & Testing
- Better separation of technical and creative tasks
- Participants familiar with system before main test
- Can fix login issues before actual data collection

---

## üìã Two-Session Structure

### SESSION 1: Login & Setup (10 minutes)

**Purpose:** Technical setup and baseline data collection

| Time | Activity | What Happens |
|------|----------|--------------|
| 0-3 min | Welcome & Consent | Sign forms, assign ID, explain two-session structure |
| 3-6 min | Questionnaire | Collect demographics and prior experience data |
| 6-9 min | Login Testing | Practice authentication, verify it works |
| 9-10 min | Schedule Session 2 | Book time for poetry session (1-7 days later) |

**Deliverables:**
- Signed consent form
- Completed questionnaire
- Verified working login
- Scheduled Session 2

---

### SESSION 2: Poetry Writing (25 minutes)

**Purpose:** Test the four room conditions and collect interaction data

| Time | Activity | What Happens |
|------|----------|--------------|
| 0-2 min | Welcome Back & Login | Participant logs in independently |
| 2-4 min | Platform Tutorial | Quick demo of chat interface and poetry forms |
| 4-19 min | **Poetry Writing** | Participant works with AI (YOU OBSERVE) |
| 19-24 min | Interview | Brief questions about experience |
| 24-25 min | Debrief & Thank You | Explain conditions, provide $25 incentive |

**Deliverables:**
- Conversation data (messages, timestamps)
- Poetry output
- Observational notes
- Interview responses

## ‚úÖ Benefits of Two-Session Approach

### 1. **Separates Technical from Creative**
- Session 1 = Technical setup
- Session 2 = Pure creative focus
- No login issues disrupting poetry writing

### 2. **Participants Come Prepared**
- Already know how to log in
- Familiar with basic interface
- Less nervousness in Session 2

### 3. **Better Data Quality**
- Session 2 focuses entirely on AI interaction
- More observation time (15 min vs. 25 min before)
- Participants can focus on creativity, not tech

### 4. **Flexible Scheduling**
- Can batch Session 1 (multiple in one day)
- Session 2 scheduled at participant convenience
- Less time pressure

### 5. **Risk Mitigation**
- Identify and fix login issues early
- Can drop participant after Session 1 if incompatible
- Technical problems don't waste creative time

### 6. **Better Participant Experience**
- Two shorter sessions vs. one long one
- Less fatigue
- Break between learning and doing
- More natural progression

## ‚ùì FAQ

**Q: Do participants get paid after Session 1 or Session 2?**
A: After completing Session 2. This ensures they return for the main data collection.

**Q: What if someone doesn't come back for Session 2?**
A: That's why Session 1 is quick and low-commitment. If they don't return, recruit a replacement. You've only invested 10 minutes.

**Q: How far apart should the two sessions be?**
A: Ideally 1-7 days. Close enough that they remember the login, but flexible for scheduling.

**Q: Can I do both sessions in one day?**
A: Not recommended. The break helps participants come fresh to the creative task. But if absolutely necessary (tight timeline), separate by at least 2-3 hours.

**Q: What if login doesn't work in Session 1?**
A: Perfect! You've discovered the issue early. Fix it before Session 2. This is exactly why we separated them.

**Q: Is the 5-minute interview long enough?**
A: Yes! We've focused on 5 essential questions. Participants are more engaged with shorter sessions. You'll still get rich insights.
