#### 3. Poetry Writing Session (15 minutes)

**Your Task:**
> Work with your AI partner to create one or more poems. Explore the collaboration. There's no "right way" to do this - just engage authentically.

### What to Know:

**About Your AI Partner (Exploratory Style):**
- Provides creative, varied suggestions
- Offers unexpected ideas and perspectives
- Explores diverse poetic possibilities
- Embraces experimentation and playfulness
- May surprise you with unconventional approaches

**You might notice:**
- AI offers multiple creative directions
- Suggestions feel more imaginative or unpredictable
- Responses vary in style and tone
- Good for exploring new creative territories
- Sometimes unexpected or surprising outputs

**Tips for Success:**

1. **Start by introducing yourself**
   - "Hi, I'd like to write a poem about [topic]"
   - "Can you help me create something creative?"

2. **Embrace the exploration**
   - Be open to unexpected suggestions
   - Try ideas you might not think of yourself
   - Follow surprising directions and see where they lead

3. **Engage in conversation**
   - Respond to AI suggestions (even unusual ones!)
   - Ask for alternatives: "Can you suggest something different?"
   - Share your reactions: "That's interesting!" or "That's not quite what I meant"

4. **Experiment with forms**
   - Try different poetry types
   - Mix styles if inspired
   - It's OK to switch mid-session

5. **Be honest about your experience**
   - If something feels too random, say so
   - If you're delighted by a surprise, express that
   - If confused by a suggestion, mention it
   - Authentic reactions help us improve the system

6. **Don't worry about quality**
   - This isn't graded
   - Focus on the collaboration process
   - Exploration is more important than perfection
   - Even "weird" poems are valuable data

**What if you get stuck?**
- Ask AI for help: "I'm not sure where to go next"
- Request different approach: "Can you suggest something else?"
- Try a different form or topic
- Facilitator will gently check in but won't interrupt

**What if AI suggests something confusing?**
- **Tell the AI!** "I don't understand that suggestion"
- Ask for clarification
- Try a different direction
- Your reactions to unexpected outputs are valuable data

**Time alerts:**
- At 12 minutes: "About 3 minutes left"
- Gives you time to wrap up

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I need poetry experience?**  
A: No! We want participants with varied backgrounds. Your fresh perspective is especially valuable for exploratory AI.

**Q: What if the AI suggests something weird or confusing?**  
A: Perfect! Tell us about it in the interview. The exploratory AI is designed to offer varied suggestions - some might be unconventional. Your reactions (positive, negative, or confused) are exactly what we need to hear.

**Q: Can I ignore AI suggestions I don't like?**  
A: Absolutely! You're in control. You can redirect the AI, ask for alternatives, or take the conversation in a different direction.

**Q: What if I can't choose among too many options?**  
A: That's valuable feedback! Tell the AI you need help deciding, or just pick one and see what happens. We want to know if variety helps or hinders.

**Q: Can I write about anything?**  
A: Yes! Choose any topic that interests you. Keep it appropriate for an academic setting.

**Q: Will my poems be judged?**  
A: No! This isn't about poetry quality. We're testing whether exploratory AI supports creative collaboration.

**Q: What if I feel overwhelmed?**  
A: Take a breath, tell the AI to simplify, or ask facilitator for help. Your experience of feeling overwhelmed is important data.

**Q: Can I see what I wrote afterward?**  
A: Yes! We can provide you with your poems if you'd like them.

**Q: What if I can't make Session 2?**  
A: Contact us ASAP to reschedule. We're flexible!

**Q: Will my identity be kept private?**  
A: Yes. You're identified only as "P2" in all data. Names are removed from transcripts.

**Q: Can I withdraw?**  
A: Yes, at any time, no questions asked. We'll still provide partial compensation for your time.
