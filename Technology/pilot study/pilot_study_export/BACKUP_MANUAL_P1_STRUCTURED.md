#### 3. Poetry Writing Session (15 minutes)

**Your Task:**
> Work with your AI partner to create one or more poems. Explore the collaboration. There's no "right way" to do this - just engage authentically.

### What to Know:

**About Your AI Partner (Structured Style):**
- Provides consistent, reliable guidance
- Follows established poetry rules and conventions
- Offers structured suggestions
- Helps with form and technique
- Maintains steady tone throughout

**You might notice:**
- AI gives clear, specific suggestions
- Feedback focuses on form and structure
- Responses feel predictable and helpful
- Good for learning poetry techniques

**Tips for Success:**

1. **Start by introducing yourself**
   - "Hi, I'd like to write a poem about [topic]"
   - "Can you help me create a haiku?"

2. **Engage in conversation**
   - Respond to AI suggestions
   - Ask questions ("What's a good metaphor for loneliness?")
   - Share your ideas ("I want to write about my hometown")

3. **Experiment with forms**
   - Try different poetry types
   - See which style resonates with you
   - It's OK to switch mid-session

4. **Be honest**
   - If confused, say so
   - If AI suggestion doesn't work, express that
   - Authentic reactions help us improve the system

5. **Don't worry about quality**
   - This isn't graded
   - Focus on the collaboration process, not perfection
   - Even incomplete poems are valuable data

**What if you get stuck?**
- Ask AI for help: "I'm not sure what to write next"
- Try a different form or topic
- Facilitator will gently check in but won't interrupt

**Time alerts:**
- At 12 minutes: "About 3 minutes left"
- Gives you time to wrap up

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I need poetry experience?**  
A: No! We want participants with varied backgrounds. Your perspective is valuable regardless of experience.

**Q: What if I can't think of anything to write?**  
A: The AI will help! You can ask for topic suggestions or writing prompts. Facilitator can also gently help if you're truly stuck.

A: Yes! Choose any topic that interests you. Keep it appropriate for an academic setting.

A: No! This isn't about poetry quality. We're testing the AI collaboration system, not your writing ability.

**Q: What if I have a technical problem?**  
A: Facilitator is nearby and will help immediately. Don't hesitate to speak up.
