## ğŸ’¡ Pro Tips for Success

1. **Treat as usability test, not research study**
   - Frame expectations appropriately
   - Focus on system improvement

2. **Maximize qualitative depth**
   - Longer interviews (10 min)
   - Think-aloud during session
   - Follow-up reflections

3. **Learn from the gap between participants**
   - Fix issues after P1
   - Test fixes with P2
   - Document improvements

4. **Be transparent about N=2**
   - In reports, acknowledge limitations
   - Don't overstate findings
   - Frame as exploratory

5. **Use findings to improve**
   - Goal = better system
   - Not: prove hypotheses
   - Every insight counts!
